About Our School

Established in 1962

ZPHS Nagari (Girls) has been a cornerstone of education for young women in Chittoor district for over six decades. Our school is managed by the Local Body and located in the urban area of Nagari block.

We cater to students from Grades 6 to 10, providing a comprehensive curriculum that balances academic excellence with character development. Our dedicated faculty ensures each student receives personalized attention to unlock their full potential.

Our mission is to empower girls through quality education, fostering leadership skills and preparing them for the challenges of tomorrow.

Academic Programs

Grade 6

Building strong foundations in languages, mathematics, science, and social studies. Introduction to computer education.

Grade 7

Developing critical thinking skills with advanced curriculum in core subjects. Focus on practical applications.

Grade 8

Enhanced science curriculum with lab activities. Introduction to career guidance and skill development.

Grade 9

Preparation for board examinations begins. Special focus on mathematics and science concepts.

Grade 10

Comprehensive board exam preparation. Career counseling and guidance for higher education.
